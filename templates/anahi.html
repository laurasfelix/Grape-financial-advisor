Who we are

Anahi Perez

Anahi is a co-founder of "Grape," an innovative company that provides clients with bundles of stocks acting as ETFs for investing in private equity within the finance market. With a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Biomedical Engineering from Emory University, Anahi possesses a unique blend of technical and healthcare expertise. Initially focused on making a difference in the healthcare field through a non-profit organization she founded, her interest later shifted towards software engineering, leading her to co-found "Grape." Anahi's multidisciplinary background and passion for leveraging technology have been instrumental in shaping the company's success. Her expertise in software engineering enables her to contribute to the development of robust systems that power the creation and management of stock bundles. Additionally, her knowledge of biomedical engineering adds a valuable perspective to the company's investment strategies, particularly in analyzing opportunities in the healthcare and life sciences sectors. Anahi's entrepreneurial spirit, adaptability, and dedication make her a driving force behind the growth of "Grape" as a leading player in the finance industry.
